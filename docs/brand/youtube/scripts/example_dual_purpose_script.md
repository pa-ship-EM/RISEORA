The RISE Method is a framework I developed for sustainable self-mastery. Here's how it works.

**[NARRATION - CORE CONTENT]**:
RISE stands for four pillars:

**R - Rewire**: Your default patterns are keeping you stuck. Rewire means identifying the unconscious habits and beliefs that run your life, and deliberately replacing them with better ones. This isn't positive thinking‚Äîit's pattern recognition and interruption.

**I - Integrate**: Knowledge without action is useless. Integration means taking what you learn and embedding it into your daily life through systems and rituals. This is where the morning protocol, the evening review, the weekly reflection come in.

**S - Sustain**: Most people can change for a week. The real challenge is sustaining that change for months, years, a lifetime. Sustain means building feedback loops, accountability structures, and identity shifts that make the new behavior inevitable.

**E - Elevate**: Once you've mastered one area, you don't stop‚Äîyou elevate. You raise the standard. You apply the same framework to the next level of your life. This is the ascent.
