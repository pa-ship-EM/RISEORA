## üéØ Shorts Strategy Overview

Before writing the script, identify which moments will work as standalone shorts:

1. **Hook Short** - The opening that grabs attention (0-60s)
2. **Value Bomb Short** - A key insight or framework (30-60s)
3. **Story/Example Short** - A relatable moment or case study (30-60s)
4. **Call-to-Action Short** - Motivational closing or next step (30-45s)

## üìä Timing Guidelines

### Long-Form Video
- **Total Duration**: 3-5 minutes (optimal for retention)
- **Hook**: First 30 seconds (critical for retention)
- **Value Delivery**: 60-70% of video
- **CTA**: Final 30-45 seconds

### Shorts
- **Ideal Duration**: 30-60 seconds
- **Maximum Duration**: 90 seconds (but shorter is better)
- **Hook**: First 3 seconds (CRITICAL)
- **Value/Punchline**: Seconds 10-45
- **CTA**: Final 5-10 seconds

## üìù Script Writing Tips

1. **Write for the ear, not the eye** - Use conversational language
2. **Pause for emphasis** - Mark natural breathing points
3. **Repeat key phrases** - Aids retention and creates soundbites
4. **Use active voice** - More engaging and direct
5. **Think in soundbites** - Each sentence should be quotable
6. **Front-load value** - Don't bury the lead
7. **End with emotion** - Leave them feeling something
