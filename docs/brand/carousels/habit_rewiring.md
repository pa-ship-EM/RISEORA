## Slide 1: Cover
- **Text**: 5 Habits That Rewire Your Identity.
- **Sub-text**: The architecture of becoming.
- **Visual**: A clean, high-end lifestyle photo (e.g., a organized workspace) with a soft bronze overlay.

## Slide 2: The Core Principle
- **Text**: You don't rise to the level of your goals; you fall to the level of your systems.
- **Sub-text**: Your identity is shaped by what you do when no one is watching.

## Slide 3: Habit 1 — The Prime Hour
- **Header**: 1. The Prime Hour.
- **Body**: Dedicate the first 60 minutes of your day to silence, reflection, and growth. No phone. No noise. Just you.

## Slide 4: Habit 2 — Curated Consumption
- **Header**: 2. Curated Consumption.
- **Body**: Your mind is a product of what you feed it. Unfollow accounts that trigger comparison. Follow those that spark elevation.

## Slide 5: Habit 3 — The Discipline of "No"
- **Header**: 3. The Discipline of "No".
- **Body**: Every time you say 'No' to a distraction, you say 'Yes' to your highest self. High-value individuals protect their energy.

## Slide 6: Habit 4 — Radical Ownership
- **Header**: 4. Radical Ownership.
- **Body**: Stop blaming circumstances. Own your morning, own your focus, own your results.

## Slide 7: Habit 5 — Aesthetic Environment
- **Header**: 5. Aesthetic Environment.
- **Body**: Clarity begins in your space. A minimalist environment reduces cognitive load and allows your highest identity to emerge.

## Slide 8: Conclusion (CTA)
- **Text**: Ready to Rise?
- **Body**: Start with Habit 1 tomorrow. Share this with one person who is on their ascent.
