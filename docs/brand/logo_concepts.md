## Concept Descriptions

### 1. The Elegant Serif
- **Vibe**: Sophisticated, established, luxury.
- **Details**: Uses a high-contrast serif font in a soft gold/bronze tone on a cream background. Ideal for a brand that emphasizes mature transformation and "rising" into a premium lifestyle.

### 2. The Clean Sans-Serif
- **Vibe**: Modern, disciplined, clear.
- **Details**: A bold yet airy sans-serif wordmark in soft black on a white background. This concept reflects the "clarity and discipline" aspects of the mission.

### 3. The Abstract Elevation Symbol
- **Vibe**: Creative, calm, growth-oriented.
- **Details**: A geometric symbol that subtly suggests a sun rising or upward movement. The sage green background adds a "calm lifestyle" feel, perfect for aesthetic b-roll overlays.
